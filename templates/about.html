Are you sick & tired of getting anime suggestions like "Boku No Pico" everytime you ask your friends?
Worry not! Introducing a simple Anime Recommendation System.


How does it work?

Just type in the name of the anime you want to get similar anime recommendations of. It will give you top 10 similar anime on the basis of its genre, studio, type & score.

Please note: The anime title that you search for is case/punctuation sensitive, so if your query doesn't give you any result, the app will give you some suggestions based on similar titles. Still, if you don't find your anime in the result try searching for it's alternative name from internet.


Algorithms behind the scene

  • Cosine Similarity
  • Levenshtein Distance
